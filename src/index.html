About Us

The Atrium Studios were opened in 1998 in the Munich Westend.

Since moving to München-Obersendling in 2009, the recording studios are located in the beautiful industrial loft of a former tobacco factory totaling 480 m².

Through our in-house video production, we can fully cover the audiovisual sector.

Our clients include advertising agencies, publishers, film productions as well as industrial and commercial companies.

We realize your productions professionally, reliably and cost-efficiently.

Give us a call! We look forward to you.
